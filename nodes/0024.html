[0025] Definition 2·a (Topos).

Let $E$ be a geometric universe. A $E$-topos $X$ or a topos in $E$ is defined to be a geometric universe $\Sh{X}$ equipped with an algebraic morphism $X:E\to\Sh{X}$ whose gluing fibration $\GL{X}$ has a small separator (Definition 4.3·d [002H]).
